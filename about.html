About Us

We are a dedicated team of five passionate individuals united by a common mission: to foster sustained, inclusive, and sustainable economic growth by empowering unemployed individuals with the skills and knowledge needed to thrive in today's dynamic job market. Our organization focuses on achieving Sustainable Development Goal 8 (SDG 8), which aims to promote productive employment and decent work for all.

Mission

At SianGaLin, our mission is to bridge the gap between unemployment and opportunity. We believe that everyone deserves the chance to achieve their full potential and contribute meaningfully to society. By providing free, high-quality training programs, we equip unemployed individuals with the tools they need to secure decent work, enhance their skills, and ultimately, improve their quality of life.

Vission

At SianGaLin, we envision a world where every individual has the opportunity to reach their fullest potential through meaningful employment. Our goal is to create a sustainable future where skills and opportunities are accessible to all, fostering a thriving, inclusive society. By continually innovating our training programs and expanding our reach, we aim to empower the unemployed to become skilled professionals and contributors to their communities, ultimately enhancing the quality of life for everyone.

WHAT WE DO ?

Our core activities revolve around:


Training Programs: We offer a wide range of training programs tailored to meet the needs of the modern workforce. These include vocational training, soft skills development, digital literacy, and entrepreneurship courses. Partnerships and Collaboration: We collaborate with businesses, educational institutions, and other NGOs to create comprehensive training curricula that are relevant and effective.

Fundraising: Our team actively raises funds to ensure that all our training programs remain free and accessible to those who need them most. We organize events, run campaigns, and engage with donors who share our vision of a world where everyone has the opportunity to work in decent and productive employment. Support and Mentorship: Beyond training, we provide ongoing support and mentorship to our participants, helping them navigate their career paths and achieve long-term success.

Our Impact

Since our inception, SianGaLin has:
Trained 696969 individuals in various skills, leading to improved employment opportunities and career advancement. Partnered with 1024 local businesses to create job placement opportunities for our graduates. Organized 748 successful fundraising events, securing the necessary resources to expand our programs and reach more people.

Our Team

Our team is the heart of SianGaLin. Each member brings unique expertise and a shared commitment to making a difference. Together, we are dedicated to creating sustainable change by empowering individuals and contributing to the achievement of SDG 8.

Join Us

We invite you to join us in our mission to create a world where everyone has access to decent work and economic growth. Whether you are interested in partnering with us, donating, or volunteering, your support can make a significant impact.

Thank you for believing in our vision and helping us build a better future for all.
